Is it really free?

Yes! 100% free and open-source (MIT License). No hidden fees, no credit card required, no sign-up needed.

Do I need to install anything?

No installation required. Just open in your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. All calculations run locally in your browser. No data is sent to any server. Your financial data never leaves your computer.

Yes! MIT License allows commercial use, modification, and distribution. Use it freely for your business, clients, or consulting work.
